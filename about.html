The people behind your front desk.

Reception is a small studio in Singapore, building the 24/7 front desk that property, insurance and financial agents could never afford before.

Why we built this.

Every day, professionals lose leads to a missed message. Someone asks a question at 11pm, or while you're with a client, and by the time you reply, that lead is already sitting with someone else. Big companies solved this years ago with call centres and expensive software. Everyone else just learned to live with it.

We didn't think that was fair. So we set out to build a front desk that any professional can afford. One that answers in seconds, in your name, around the clock. It never quotes a figure or gives advice; it captures the lead and routes it to you, so far fewer slip away.

No call centre. No app for you to babysit. Just a front desk that works while you get on with the actual job.

Adam
Founder, Reception · Singapore

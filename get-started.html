Questions people ask.

How long until it's live?

Usually within a few minutes of our first chat. We set it all up and show you before it goes live.

What does it cost?

The front desk is S$199 a month as a founding-member rate if you join by 31 October, then S$249, set up for you, no hidden fees and no lock-in. Websites and complex AI systems are quoted separately. See the pricing page for the full picture.

Do I pay upfront?

No. Tell us your basics and we build your front desk for you to try, free. You talk to it yourself, and you only pay when you're happy and ready to go live.

How do I manage it?

From one simple page. Change your prices, hours, services and answers yourself anytime, or message us and we'll do it for you. Changes go live on the next customer.

Does it really speak Mandarin?

Yes. When a customer writes in Mandarin, it replies and speaks back in a natural Mandarin voice on its own. You don't set anything up.

What if it says something wrong?

It only answers from what you give it, your real prices, services and hours. If it doesn't know something, it never guesses, it takes the customer's details and passes them to you.

Where do my enquiries go?

Straight to your Telegram or WhatsApp the moment someone leaves their details, each with a short summary of what they want, and they're saved on your leads page too. Nothing is sold or shared.

The simple terms.

What you get, what it costs, and what to expect. No fine print traps.

Last updated 15 July 2026

What Reception is

Reception sets up and runs a front desk for your business: an assistant that answers your customers on your own link and QR code, in your brand, and passes their enquiries to you. We may also build websites, custom tools and complex AI automation systems where you ask us to.

Reception is a service of Reception SG (UEN 53528300L), a business registered in Singapore. In these terms, "we" and "us" mean Reception SG.

What it costs

The front desk is S$249 a month. It is one flat price, with no hidden fees and no setup charge. Websites, custom tools and complex AI automation systems are quoted separately before any work starts, so you always know the price up front.

The front desk is for normal business use and comfortably handles everyday customer volume. There are no per-message fees. To keep the service fast and fair for everyone, unusually high or automated traffic may be rate-limited, but in ordinary use you will never come close to these limits.

No lock-in

There is no contract and no lock-in. You can cancel any time and your front desk will be switched off at the end of the paid month. We will not hold your enquiries hostage, you can ask for an export before you go.

How the front desk answers

The front desk answers from the information you give us, your services, prices, hours and any notes. It is built to never invent a price or a fact, and to take a customer's details when it does not know something. Like any assistant it can occasionally get something wrong, so you are responsible for keeping the information you give it accurate, and for the prices, claims and promises it passes on as yours.

Using it properly

  • Use the service for a genuine business, and follow the law that applies to your trade.
  • Do not use it to mislead customers, or to collect information you are not entitled to.
  • For regulated work, such as property or insurance, the front desk shares general information and takes details for your licensed person to follow up. It does not give regulated advice, and neither do we.

Keeping it running

We work hard to keep the service available around the clock, and to fix any problems quickly. We cannot promise it will never be interrupted, for example during provider outages or maintenance, but your enquiries are saved to your dashboard so nothing is lost if a notification is delayed.

Fair limits

To the extent the law allows, Reception is provided as is, and our responsibility to you is limited to the fees you paid us in the month a problem happened. We are not responsible for lost business or indirect losses. Nothing here removes rights you have that cannot be removed under Singapore law.

Your customers' data

How we handle the personal details your customers leave is set out in our privacy page. In short, for those enquiries we act on your behalf, we do not sell the data, and we pass it to you.

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of Singapore, and any dispute is dealt with in the Singapore courts.

Contact us

Anything unclear? Message us on WhatsApp or email hello@reception.sg. We would rather answer a question early than surprise you later.
